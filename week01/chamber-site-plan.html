São Paulo Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The São Paulo Chamber of Commerce supports business growth by providing networking opportunities, resources, and advocacy for companies of all sizes.

We aim to foster economic development by connecting local entrepreneurs with national and international markets.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events will facilitate business networking in São Paulo?
  • Where can I find the contact information of the chamber's board members?
  • What are the latest statistics on São Paulo's business growth?

Color Scheme

Dark Blue (#005f73): Used for headers and navigation.

Teal (#0a9396): Accent color for buttons and highlights.

Light Gray (#f4f4f4): Background color for readability.

Typography

Roboto: Used for all body text for clarity.

Montserrat: Used for headings to create a professional feel.

Wireframe (Layout Plan)

The home page will have a header, navigation menu, main content section with multiple cards, and a footer. A mobile-first design will be implemented, ensuring responsiveness with larger screen adaptations.
